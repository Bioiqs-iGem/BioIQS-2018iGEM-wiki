In our project, we followed 3 different experimental lines:

Obtention of the HLA-DQ gene from a DNA sample of a celiac patient.

Synthetic constructs for E.coli

Synthetic constructs for P.pastoris.

Different members conducted each of these parts. To avoid misunderstandings, we kept track of the work done every week in three different notebooks.
